
# **Roadmap & Growth Strategy**
### **Roadmap**
**Phase 1 – MVP Development (0-6 Months)**
- Develop & launch the mobile application.
- Establish retail partnerships to integrate the platform.
- Initiate NFT sales & token distribution.

**Phase 2 – Expansion (6-12 Months)**
- Scale to major cities and high-traffic retail hubs.
- Introduce community engagement features to increase adoption.
- Integrate token staking & premium rewards.

**Phase 3 – Global Adoption (12-24 Months)**
- Partner with enterprise retail chains to expand reach.
- Launch in international markets.
- Onboard millions of active users.

### **Market Penetration Strategy**
- **Retail Incentive Campaigns**: Encouraging businesses to adopt Goku Miner.
- **User Growth Hack Strategies**: Leveraging referrals and social engagement.
- **Partnerships with influencers & gaming communities**: Driving awareness and engagement.
