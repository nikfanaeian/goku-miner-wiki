
# **4. Business Model**
### **Revenue Streams**
1. **NFT Sales** – Retail partners purchase NFT-powered locations to join the network.
2. **Mining Activity Fees** – Users & businesses contribute to transactions for accessing mining features.
3. **Token Exchange Fees** – Transactions for redeemable assets generate additional revenue.
4. **Business Subscriptions** – Premium listings & analytics services offer businesses enhanced features.
5. **Advertising & Promotions** – Sponsored mining locations allow brands to increase visibility.

### **Value Chain**
- **Technology Development**: Building and maintaining the app, blockchain integration, and smart contracts.
- **Market Operations**: Acquiring users and onboarding business partners.
- **Customer Support**: Providing education, analytics, and loyalty program advisory services.
